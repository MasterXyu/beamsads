All the news

Roscoe.TV is built to bring together news junkies, casual journalists, and media pros locally. We're currently in beta release, offering content for New York and San Francisco.

We plan to add coverage for other cities as well soon. If you'd like to be contacted via email when we have a release ready for your hometown, please sign up for our mailing list.

We've built our beta version as a "Web app," a piece of software that runs right in your browser. It features a responsive design, meaning that it should display well on devices of all sizes, including smartphones, tablets, or personal computers.

Roscoe.TV is a product of Roscoe Labs, a founder-led, mobile-focused news startup. If you notice a bug, or have other feedback, please do drop us a message so we can make the app even better.

We also invite users to follow us on Twitter and Facebook.

Our snail-mail address is Roscoe Labs; 317 Washington Street, Suite 151; Oakland, CA 94607.
